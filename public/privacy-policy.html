Privacy Policy — Camp Mantap

Last updated: July 2026

Camp Mantap ("we", "us", "our") operates an automated messaging assistant ("the Service") on WhatsApp to help respond to customer inquiries. This policy explains what information the Service processes and how it is handled.

1. What We Process

When you message Camp Mantap on WhatsApp, the Service reads the content of your message in order to generate and send an automated reply. This includes:

  • The text of the message you send us
  • Your WhatsApp phone number, as needed to send the reply back to you

2. What We Do Not Do

The Service does not retain or store your messages, phone number, or any conversation history after a reply has been generated and sent. Each message is processed only for the purpose of producing an immediate response, and is not saved to a database or log for later use, profiling, or analysis.

3. Third-Party Processing

To generate replies, your message content is passed to WhatsApp's messaging infrastructure (operated by Meta) and to a third-party AI service (Google's Gemini/Vertex AI) solely to produce a response. These providers may process the data transiently in accordance with their own privacy policies:

  • WhatsApp Privacy Policy
  • Google Privacy Policy

4. No Sale or Sharing of Data

We do not sell, rent, or share your information with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only passed to the service providers listed above, and only as needed to deliver a reply to you.

5. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed at children, and we do not knowingly process messages from individuals under the age required by WhatsApp's own terms of service.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time as the Service evolves. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
