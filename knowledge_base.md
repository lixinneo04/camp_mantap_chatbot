# CAMP MANTAP OFFICIAL KNOWLEDGE BASE

## About Us & Services
I am the virtual WhatsApp assistant for Camp Mantap.
Camp Mantap is a premium campsite located around 20-25 minutes drive from Bentong, Pahang, where all our campsites face a beautiful river.

We provide:
- Riverfront camping sites (all sites face the river)
- 24-hour electricity plug points at each campsite
- Firepits at each campsite
- Toilets with water heaters (+soap) and washing areas
- Self-service Mini Mart (selling ice, firewood, charcoal, snacks, drinks, etc.)
- Surau and close-by car parking
- WiFi (Celcom/Digi)
- Guided ATV tours (RM 70 per car), archery, and other seasonal activities

## Check-In & Check-Out Times
- Official Check-In: 2:00 PM
- Official Check-Out: 12:00 PM (noon)

## Cancellation, Refund & Rescheduling Policies
### Cancellation & Refund Policy
- More than 14 days before check-in: 100% Refund
- 14 to 7 days before check-in: 50% Refund
- Less than 7 days before check-in: NO Refund

### Rescheduling Policy
- Notice must be given more than 14 days before the check-in date.
- The new date must be within 1 month of the original check-in date.

## Electricity Usage Policy
- Electricity is suitable for basic usage such as phone charging, fan, hair dryer & rice cooker (below 1000 watts).
- Do not use high-power appliances.
- NOT ALLOWED: Charging EV (electric vehicle) cars.
- NOT ALLOWED: Portable Power Stations.

## ATV Ride & Archery Activities
- Guided ATV tours only: 45 minutes duration.
- Pricing: RM 70.00 per car.
- Weight limits: Max 90kg for 125cc ATVs, max 110kg for 180cc ATVs.

## Camper Van, Motorhome & RV Policy
- Camper vans, Motorhomes, and RVs are NOT recommended/suitable at Camp Mantap due to:
  - Single-phase power supply limit (insufficient for large vehicle requirements).
  - Narrow access roads, uneven terrain, and clearance challenges.
  - Durian tree low clearance (risk of scratches/damage).
  - Wet weather conditions.

## River & Flood Safety
- During heavy rain, water levels can rise significantly (historical high of 7 feet).
- The camp compound is built 10 feet above the riverbed, and water has not overflowed into our compound.
- We cannot predict natural conditions perfectly, so we have installed a warning siren system and monitor the river closely when it rains, even in the early hours.
- Semasa hujan lebat, paras air akan meningkat dengan ketara dan kami telah melihat paras tertinggi iaitu 7 kaki. Perkarangan kami berada pada 10 kaki di atas dan setakat ini kami tidak menemui air melimpah ke kawasan kami.
